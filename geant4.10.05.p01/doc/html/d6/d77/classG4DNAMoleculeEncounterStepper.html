Detailed Description

Given a molecule G4DNAMoleculeEncounterStepper will calculate for its possible reactants what will be the minimum encounter time and the associated molecules.

  • This model includes dynamical time steps as explained in "Computer-Aided Stochastic Modeling of the Radiolysis of Liquid Water", V. Michalik, M. Begusov√°, E. A. Bigildeev, Radiation Research, Vol. 149, No. 3 (Mar., 1998), pp. 224-236
